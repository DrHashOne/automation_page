Our Core Automation Solutions

1. Mill Automation

Mill automation transforms sugarcane milling into a high-performance, intelligently managed process. From automated cane feeding to precise juice flow stabilization, every function is optimized for maximum efficiency, safety, and reliability all with minimal manual intervention.

2. Boiler Automation

Boiler automation brings advanced control and real-time monitoring to the boiler house boosting efficiency, safety, and reliability. Featuring intelligent multi-element control and fully automated feeding systems, it ensures smooth, stable operations with full visibility and minimal manual intervention.

3. Falling Film Evaporators (FFE) Automation

Experience next-level efficiency with FFE (Falling Film Evaporator) automation designed to take control of critical process parameters for consistent and optimized evaporation. From maintaining precise evaporator levels to managing inlet and recirculation flows, and even activating hot water during emergencies, intelligent automation minimizes manual intervention while maximizing performance.

4. Pan Automation

Pan Automation revolutionizes the sugar boiling process by automating the control of critical parameters in vacuum pans such as steam pressure, vacuum levels, and massecuite level. The result? Consistent product quality, improved energy efficiency, and reduced dependence on manual operations.

5. PRDS Automation

PRDS (Pressure Reducing and Desuperheating Station) automation ensures precise delivery of steam at the right pressure and temperature customized for various process requirements across the plant. By automatically adjusting Superheated steam parameters, it enhances efficiency, safety, and process reliability.

6. Condenser Automation

Condenser automation generates the required vacuum efficiently using only the optimum amount of water and power. By intelligently monitoring the temperature difference between vapor and condensate, as well as adapting to fluctuating injection water temperatures due to ambient conditions, it ensures stable vacuum performance with reduced resource consumption.

7. Melter Automation

Melter automation brings precision and efficiency to the raw sugar melting process. Designed for sugar refineries, it ensures consistent melt quality while minimizing energy consumption and maximizing process control‚Äîdelivering smooth, reliable operation with less manual effort.

8. pH Automation

pH automation ensures precise pH control throughout the juice clarification process‚Äîguaranteeing pure, crystal-white sugar every time. With a proven multi-loop control at both the lime and sulphitation stages, and automated lime dosing directly proportional to juice flow and final pH, it delivers unmatched consistency and efficiency.

9. Centrifugal Feed Automation

Centrifugal feed automation ensures accurate & consistent feeding to centrifugal machines, optimizing separation efficiency while reducing manual oversight. Automated controls maintain ideal feed rates, improving product quality and enhancing operational stability.

10. Steam Head Sugar Silo Automation

Steam Head Sugar Silo automation optimizes the handling and discharge of sugar from silos, ensuring smooth flow while preventing blockages and product degradation. Automated controls maintain ideal temperature and moisture levels, preserving Sugar quality and streamlining unloading operations.

Online Monitoring Systems

Ensure full environmental compliance and real-time data insight across critical infrastructure with our integrated online monitoring solutions, as per CPCB and SPCB norms.

System Capabilities

  • Online Waste Water Monitoring System
  • Online Emission Monitoring System
  • Online Ambient Air Quality Monitoring System
  • Telemetry Flow Meters (as per CGWA norms)
  • Piezometers (as per CGWA norms)
  • Seamless Online Connectivity with CPCB & SPCB Servers

Key Monitoring Areas

  • 1. Effluent & Sewage Monitoring: Continuous monitoring of water quality at treatment plant outlets. Automated data sharing with CPCB/SPCB servers.
  • 2. Emission Monitoring: Stack-mounted sensors for continuous dust and gas emission tracking. Real-time readings in mg/Nm3 or kg/hr to meet pollution norms.
  • 3. Ambient Air Monitoring (AQMS): Tracks air quality: SO2, NOx, CO, O3, PM, etc. Also measures temperature, wind, pressure, and rainfall.
  • 4. Telemetry Flow Meters: Cloud-based flow measurement as per CGWA norms. Real-time usage data for better water management.
  • 5. Piezometers: Monitor groundwater level and quality (TDS, pH, EC). Supports CGWA compliance and water governance.
